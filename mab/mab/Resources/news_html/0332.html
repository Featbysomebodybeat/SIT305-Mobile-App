Coronavirus updates LIVE: Morrison government to end childcare relief package as Black Lives Matter protests increase risk of COVID-19 infections, Australian death toll stands at 102

If you suspect you or a family member has coronavirus you should call (not visit) your GP or ring the national Coronavirus Health Information Hotline on 1800 020 080.

Five new coronavirus cases have been recorded in Australia today: three in New South Wales and two in Victoria. There were no new cases confirmed in Queensland today.

There are now more than 7 million COVID-19 confirmed cases worldwide. The global death toll now stands at more than 402,000, according to the Johns Hopkins University tally.

Australia's free childcare scheme, introduced as a pandemic financial relief measure, will end on July 12.

Finance Minister Mathias Cormann has said the tens of thousands of people who protested against Indigenous Australian deaths in custody were selfish and risked sparking a second coronavirus outbreak.
